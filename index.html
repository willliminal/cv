Will Hams

Founder and CEO of Liminal Wellbeing.

Will Hams was born in Sale on the 14th of July 1994. Growing up as a keen sportsmna, will went on to play in the AFL for the Essendon Football Club after getting drafter in 2012. Following a 4 year stint in the AFL, Will went on to a serious of jobs before finding his feet at Student Travel Company. Due to the shortcomings of COVID-19 Will found himself a gap in the market supporting shifts in education where the importance of wellbeing was missed. This started the journey of Liminal, in itself a journey of transition and discovery while helping individuals to do the same.
